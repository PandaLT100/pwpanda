Intro To HTML

What is HTML?

HTML, HyperText Markup Language, is a programming language that is known as the foundation of a website. HTML does nothing but add text, lists, pop-ups, and boxes (undecorated) onto your website. Without the other languages to support it, your website will look like just a bunch of headings, paragraphs, and lists.

Why Use HTML?

Why not? HTML is the foundation language of all websites. Websites need all 3 languages in order to function properly. Even if it doesn't have any backend.
